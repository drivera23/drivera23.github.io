Constructing 2D Watermarks by Composition
Alcibiades Bustillo, Diego Rivera Correa
SIDIM XXXVIII, 2023
abstract / image

This study presented a method of constructing 2D periodic arrays by composing a 1D periodic array. These arrays resulted in watermarks that were robust against many image-manipulating strategies.
