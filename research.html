Research

My research experiences have been mostly centered around Visualization, Databases, HCI, and Statistical Methods. The projects here are organized in reverse chronological order. As of right now, my research is centered around graph databases. More specifically, I am interested in understanding how non-trivial graph pattern queries are executed, and if current systems implement solutions that involve producing huge intermediate results, whereas the final output size is much smaller. I hope to keep working on them and be able to optimize for scalable solutions.

Constructing 2D Watermarks by Composition
Alcibiades Bustillo , Diego Rivera Correa
SIDIM XXXVIII, 2023
abstract / image

This study presented a method of constructing 2D periodic arrays by composing a 1D periodic array. These arrays resulted in watermarks that were robust against many image-manipulating strategies.
